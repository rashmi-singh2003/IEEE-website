About Us

The IEEE Women In Engineering (WIE) Student Branch DEI is a community of dynamic individuals working towards promoting women engineers and scientists to achieve unprecedented goals, and inspiring girls around the globe to nurture, cultivate and follow their academic interests in a career in engineering, science and technology. The WIE Student Branch DEI encourages its cause by organising various events like seminars, webinars, workshops and competitions to boost the interest of people, especially young girls, in the up-and-coming areas of the various sciences. It also provides students with access to the IEEE WIE Newsletter, along with opportunities to get their hands on WIE awards, scholarships, and travel grants.

What is WIE Soiciety?

IEEE WIE is one of the biggest forces on the planet to drive the change in the world of engineering, science and technology. It plays a major role in making the way for, and encouraging women to forge their own paths and make history. This global network of students, professionals and experts brings together more than 20,000 members from more than 100 countries to advance women in technology, at all stages of their lives and careers. IEEE WIE offers its members the opportunity to connect with one another, gain prestigious mentors, and work together with the aim of benefitting humanity at large.

vision & mission

The mission is to be an agent of change, promote inclusivity and diversity, and provide one and all with a network and community to gain endless knowledge about their academic areas of interest, which in turn will help in facilitating their professional journey throughout life.
The vision is to facilitate the development of programs and activities that promote and widen the scope for women in technical disciplines across the globe, so as to have equal opportunities for all people, irrespective of their gender.


IEEE SB DEI

Dayalbagh, Agra, Uttar Pradesh 282005.
